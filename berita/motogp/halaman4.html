Halaman 4 - MOTO GP

Divizioso: the attendance king

The Italian, along with teammate Jorge Lorenzo, has been competing on the world satage for 16 straight years - the third highest on the grid behind Valentino Rossi (Movistar Yamaha MotoGP) and Dani Pedrosa (Repsol Honda Team). It's a huge testament to the 32-years-old, who in the last two years has been - on paper - Mark Marquez' (Repsol Honda Team) most consistent challenger.
